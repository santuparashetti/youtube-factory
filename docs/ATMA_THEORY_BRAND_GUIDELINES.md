# Atma Theory --- Brand Guidelines for Future Videos

> **Mission:** Ancient Wisdom for Modern Life

The goal of every video is to make timeless philosophical ideas feel
immediately relevant through cinematic storytelling, curiosity, and
memorable visual branding.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Brand Identity

Every video should feel like it belongs to the same universe.

Core attributes:

-   Timeless
-   Premium
-   Cinematic
-   Thought-provoking
-   Calm, not sensational
-   Deep but accessible

Avoid trends, memes, clickbait faces, and bright colors.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Visual Language

## Color Palette

-   Black
-   Gold
-   Warm amber
-   White text

Maintain the same cinematic lighting and contrast across every
thumbnail.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Thumbnail Formula

## Rule #1

**Thumbnail asks a question.**

Examples:

-   WHO OWNS WHO?
-   ARE YOU LIVING?
-   WHO CONTROLS YOU?
-   WHAT ARE YOU CHASING?
-   WHY ARE YOU AFRAID?
-   WHAT IS REAL?
-   WHY DO YOU SUFFER?
-   WHO ARE YOU?
-   WHAT LASTS FOREVER?
-   WHAT ARE YOU SEEKING?

Keep the main headline to **3--6 words**.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Title Formula

## Rule #2

**Title delivers a bold statement or timeless truth.**

Examples:

-   Nobody Owns Anything
-   Success Is a Trap
-   Fear Is Learned
-   You Are Being Controlled
-   The World Lied To You
-   Everything You Chase Will Leave
-   Happiness Was Never the Goal
-   Peace Was Always Within You
-   Nothing Outside Can Complete You
-   Attachment Creates Suffering
-   Freedom Begins With Letting Go
-   The Ego Is the Prison
-   You Already Have Enough
-   The Greatest Illusion Is Control

The title should complement the thumbnail---not repeat it.

Example:

Thumbnail: \> ARE YOU LIVING?

Title: \> Success Is a Trap

This creates an information gap that encourages clicks.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Numbered Wisdom Videos

When teaching philosophy or historical thinkers:

Examples:

-   4 Truths That Will Change You
-   5 Lessons From Buddha
-   7 Stoic Principles
-   3 Ancient Secrets
-   10 Timeless Truths

Avoid long descriptive titles.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Thumbnail Guidelines

Use:

-   One powerful central subject
-   Strong silhouette or portrait
-   Gold rim lighting
-   Minimal distractions
-   High contrast

Prefer:

-   Monk
-   Philosopher
-   Solitary person
-   Ancient statue
-   Mountains
-   Temple
-   Tree
-   Cosmic imagery

Avoid:

-   Busy layouts
-   Tiny objects
-   Multiple focal points
-   Excessive text

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Typography

Maximum:

-   4--6 major words

Highlight one or two keywords using gold.

Examples:

GOOD

WHO OWNS WHO?

GOOD

THE SILENT FORCE

Avoid paragraphs of text.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Video Topics

Stay focused on timeless philosophy.

Core themes:

-   Identity
-   Ego
-   Attachment
-   Desire
-   Fear
-   Anxiety
-   Happiness
-   Freedom
-   Death
-   Time
-   Karma
-   Consciousness
-   Purpose
-   Inner Peace
-   Wisdom
-   Success
-   Detachment

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Tone

Every video should feel like:

> "An ancient teacher explaining modern life."

Not:

-   Motivation
-   Life hacks
-   Hustle culture
-   Self-help clichés

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Quality Checklist

Before publishing, ask:

-   Does the thumbnail ask one compelling question?
-   Does the title state one powerful truth?
-   Do they complement each other?
-   Can the thumbnail be understood in under one second?
-   Is the biggest word readable on mobile?
-   Does it look unmistakably like an Atma Theory video?
-   Would I click this if I had never heard of the channel?

If every answer is **Yes**, publish.
