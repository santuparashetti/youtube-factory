# ATMA THEORY MASTER THUMBNAIL CONTEXT v2

## Core Identity

Create YouTube thumbnails that instantly communicate deep philosophical
insight with a premium cinematic editorial look.

## Canvas

-   1672 × 941 pixels
-   16:9
-   High contrast

## Default Background

Use a light warm ivory / cream / parchment background with a subtle
golden tint. Never use pure white or gray.

## Colors

-   Black
-   Rich Gold

No extra accent colors unless requested.

## Layout

-   Text on left (45%)
-   Subject on right (55%)
-   Lots of negative space

## Typography

-   Huge condensed bold font
-   3--8 words
-   Black + Gold only
-   Distressed texture

## Subject

Single symbolic cinematic subject only. Examples: - Human silhouette -
Philosopher statue - Mask - Meditating person

Minimal symbolic props only.

## Lighting

Warm golden rim light. Soft cinematic glow.

## Composition

Clean. Minimal. Premium. Editorial.

## Logo

Small Atma Theory logo at bottom-left.

-   WHO OWNS WHO?
-   ARE YOU LIVING?

-   PLATO'S 2 THINGS TO FORGET & 2 TO REMEMBER

Every future thumbnail should match this visual language unless
