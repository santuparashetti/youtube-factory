# Atma Theory Thumbnail Design Context (Master Guide)

## Channel Identity

-   Style: Cinematic oil painting mixed with illustrated realism.
-   Theme: Ancient wisdom for modern life.
-   Audience: English-speaking (primarily US).
-   Goal: High CTR with timeless branding.

## Main Character (Never Change Identity)

The recurring character is the face of the channel.

Keep consistent: - Curly messy hair - Round black glasses - Average
relatable build - Slight stubble - Everyday appearance - Looks like an
ordinary seeker, not a guru - Warm expressive face

Vary: - Pose - Expression - Camera angle - Clothing layers (hoodie,
jacket, blazer, sweater) - Environment - Lighting

Avoid always showing: - Backpack - Standing in same pose - Looking
upward

## Character Pose Ideas

-   Pointing at viewer
-   Sitting on stairs
-   Walking into light
-   Looking into mirror
-   Holding old book
-   Breaking chains
-   Climbing mountain
-   Opening ancient door
-   Writing
-   Looking over shoulder
-   Crossing bridge
-   Holding socks (billionaire story)
-   Holding empty bird cage
-   Standing in rain
-   Running
-   Smiling slightly
-   Shocked
-   Determined
-   Relieved
-   Side profile
-   Front close-up
-   Three-quarter portrait

Treat the character like an actor.

## Background Rules

Do NOT always use: - Golden sunset - Mountain path - Black background

Rotate environments: - Library - Museum - Busy city - Subway - Roman
ruins - Temple courtyard - Cathedral - Forest - Desert - Ocean cliff -
Snow landscape - Japanese alley - Rooftop - Chess room - Old
marketplace - Abandoned factory - Storm - Blue hour - Emerald forest -
Deep red dramatic sky - Muted teal interiors

## Color Strategy

Rotate palettes: - Blue + White - Burgundy + Cream - Emerald + White -
Slate + Cyan - Rust + Ivory - Forest Green + Beige - Red + White -
Orange + Navy - Teal + Cream

Avoid making every thumbnail gold.

## Typography

Use 2--4 words. Examples: - DON'T SETTLE - BREAK THE CYCLE - THE WORRY
TRAP - THE NEXT CHAPTER - WHO OWNS WHO - THE HIDDEN COST - WORDS SHAPE
YOUR FATE

Typography rules: - Large and readable on mobile - White is primary -
Accent color changes by scene - Mix modern + timeless serif/sans look -
Experiment with layouts: - Huge single word - Vertical stack - Diagonal
composition - Text behind character - Character overlapping text

## Composition

One clear focal point. High contrast. Minimal clutter. Emotion before
detail.

## Thumbnail Review Checklist

-   Is the pose different from the last thumbnail?
-   Is the emotion unique?
-   Is the background fresh?
-   Is the accent color different?
-   Is the typography layout varied?
-   Is the character instantly recognizable?
-   Can the thumbnail be understood in under one second?

## Long-Term Brand Goal

Create a recognizable visual language where viewers identify Atma Theory
thumbnails instantly without reading the channel name.
